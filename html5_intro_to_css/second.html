Eating Healthy and Being Active

Is it really that important?

Just as with anyone, it's important to eat a healthy diet and be active to keep our bodies working well. This doesn't mean to go on a strict diet and work out for hours every day. However, one should try to eat fruits and vegetables and limit the amount of sugar they're eating. Also, getting some kind of physical activity is also important. Whether that's going on a run, taking a walk with friends, or even doing some strength training, working some sort of physical activity into your day is a good thing. However, this doesn't mean that breaks aren't allowed. It's okay to skip your workout and have that piece of cake every now and then!

Some Misconceptions of Type One Diabetes

The More You Know!

There are some misconceptions out there that people believe about type one diabetes. However, they aren't true. The linked article above lists several. One, for example, is that people with this condition developed it from eating too much sugar when that just isn't the case. You can't get this disease from eating too much sugar. Another is that type one can be cured; however, there currently is no cure for this disease. Last, some people think that type one diabetics can't eat sugar. Again, this isn't the case. For me, I can eat whatever I like as long as I give the appropriate amount of insulin. So next time you see a type one diabetic eating a cookie or a piece of cake, don't tell them they can't have it. As long as they're doing as they should, it'll all be okay!
