Basic Information of Type One Diabetes

What is Diabetes?

Type one diabetes is an autoimmune disease that causes the pancreas to stop making insulin for the body. Becasue of this, those with this condition must take insulin through shots or an insulin pump. Just giving insulin is far from what all is needed to live a healthy life. Glucose management is also needed. Some do this by pricking their fingers. However, some, including myself, use a continuous glucose monitor, such as a Dexcom G7. This small device is worn on the body and sends signals to a device, like a smartphone, to read your glucose levels. It helps make management easier, constantly knowing what your glucose readings are. Of course, like anyone, eating healthy foods and getting exercise is also important.

How Life is Impacted

A Basic Need-To-Know

Life is, without a doubt, impacted due to type one diabetes. Because of this disease, you now have to think about prescriptions and doctor appointments. If you're away from home, you need to make sure you have something or can get to something if your blood sugar gets too low. However, when managed well, people with type one diabetes can live pretty normal lives. Of course, there are things to be cautious of. For example, one should know how certain foods affect their blood sugar. They should know how exercise affects their blood sugar as well. This disease is something you have to take charge of. If not, it can lead to serious consequences.
