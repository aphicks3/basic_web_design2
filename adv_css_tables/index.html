J. R. R. Tolkien - The Genius Behind Middle Earth

If you've never seen The Lord of the Rings or The Hobbit, first of all, why haven't you? And second of all, you definitely should! Like right now! Go now! These movies are absolutely amazing, but it wouldn't have been possible without the creative writing of J. R. R. Tolkien. This brilliant man authored The Lord of the Rings trilogy and The Hobbit. Besides reading a bit from The Hobbit, I, myself, haven't read them. Although, I absolutely would love to read them one day.

The One Ring

The One Ring plays a role in both sets of movies. In The Hobbit movies, it sets up the larger role it plays in The Lord of the Rings. With this ring comes power but also darkness. A dark control is placed upon the one who has possession of it or sometimes one who wants possession of it. This is seen in several people, including Bilbo Baggins and Frodo Baggins. If you have no clue what I'm talking about, that's all the more reason to go watch these movies!
