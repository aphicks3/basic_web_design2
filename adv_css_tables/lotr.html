The Lord of the Rings

The Lord of the Rings films are beautiful films. They tell a story of adventure. They show the duality of triumph and loss, of love and heartbreak. The first movie opens in the Shire, home of the Hobbits, where soon the young Frodo Baggins will go on a dangerous mission. Some notable characters of these films include Frodo, Samwise Gamgee, Aragorn the Ranger, Gandalf the Grey, Legolas Greenleaf, and many others. The first movie begins the Fellowship, consisting of four Hobbits, two Men, a Wizard, an Elf, and a Dwarf.

The Triumphant, yet Heartbreaking End

The One Ring must be taken to Mordor, and Frodo and Sam make their way to accomplish this dangerous and deadly mission. The two friends complete the goal save Middle Earth from the evil Sauron. However, after going through what they went through, it forever changed them. Frodo decides to leave his friends and the Shire and sail into the Grey Havens where most, if not all, the Elves have gone. This heartbreaking decision was one Frodo believed he needed to make. At the end of the movie, Sam is seen in the Shire with his wife and children, living a peaceful and happy life now that the evil in Middle Earth has been vanquished.
