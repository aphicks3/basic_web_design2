Cookies, Anyone?

Who doesn't love a soft, warm chocolate chip cookie? Cookies can be one of the easier things to bake. Find a recipe you love and practice it. Keep practicing until you feel you've mastered that recipe. Maybe down the line you may want to experiment with it by adding certain ingredients or changing the quantity of some others. See what happens!
