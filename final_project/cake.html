Cake, Cake, Cake!

Baking a cake can be simple, but decorating a cake can be where it gets more complicated than cookies. However, if you're in a rush, or you're not that confident in yourself yet to bake a cake from scratch, there is a solution! Box cake. You may be thinking, "Box cake? Are you serious?" But yes, I am! Becasue there are ways you can fancify your average box cake mix. For example, you can add another egg than what the box calls for. You can replace the water with milk, and you can switch the oil for butter. For more information, see this website: Box Cake Improvement
