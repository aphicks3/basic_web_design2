Some Decoration Examples

The Challenge of Decoration

Decoration is something that takes practice. Sometimes, however decorating can be very simple. It can be as simple as some frosting and sprinkles. Or, it could be a bit more difficult, such as iced sugar or shortbread cookies. Or, it could be even more difficult, such as filling, frosting, and decorating a wedding cake. It can be daunting, but like I said, with practice, it can become easier.
