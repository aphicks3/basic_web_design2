The Hobbit

The Hobbit trilogy is a prequel to the Lord of the Rings trilogy. It begins sixty years before Frodo and the Fellowship begin their journey. These movies follow Bilbo Baggins, a relative of Frodo Baggins. These movies tell a story of adventure as well. They show Bilbo joining a Company of Dwarves to help them regain their home from the enourmous Dragon, Smaug. However, more danger awaits them than just a dragon.

Notable Characters From The Hobbit

Thorin Oakenshield The Company of Dwarves

Another Heatbreaking End

What would these movies be without heartbreak? As the movies continue, Bilbo grows to care for the thirteen Dwarves he's traveling with. However, three of his new friends don't make it out of the ending battle alive. Bilbo, the Hobbit used to comfort and home, has seen death, and not just death, but the loss of good friends. He becomes a changed Hobbit after the adventure is over. Bilbo finally makes it back home, and the end of the movie jumps to present time when The Lord of the Rings is beginning.
